Help - How to Use the Survey Application

Getting Started

To use this application, you need to create an account and log in.

Creating Surveys

  1. After logging in, click "Create New Survey" on your dashboard
  2. Enter a title and description for your survey
  3. Add up to 5 response options
  4. Click "Create Survey" to save

Sharing Surveys

Once created, you can share the survey link with respondents. They don't need an account to respond.

Viewing Results

From your dashboard, click "View Results" next to any survey to see response statistics.

Managing Surveys

You can activate/deactivate surveys from your dashboard. Inactive surveys cannot receive new responses.
